How Digital Savings Circles Work

1

Create Your Circle

Choose a circle type, set a goal, and invite members. Give it a meaningful name like "Mwaba Family School Fund".

2

Set Community Agreements

Use our templates to create fair rules for contributions, decision-making, and withdrawals. Everyone agrees transparently.

3

Save Together Digitally

Members contribute via mobile money (directly to each other). The platform tracks all contributions transparently for everyone to see.

4

Achieve Goals & Grow

Watch your community savings grow. When goals are met, funds are distributed according to your community agreements.
