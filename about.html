FAQ

Is Tulo Square a bank or investment platform?

No. We're a community coordination platform. We don't hold funds or offer investments.

How does money move on Tulo Square?

Money moves directly between users via mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Money) or bank transfers. We track the commitments.

Do I need to be in a large group to use Tulo Square?

No. Start with family or a few friends. The platform works for any size group.

Is Tulo Square registered with authorities?

Yes, we're registered with PACRA as a technology platform for community coordination.

What if there's a dispute in our savings circle?

We provide templates for community agreements and dispute resolution, but the community manages its own affairs.
